About The Cargo Crew

Trusted Shipping Nation Wide

The Cargo Crew is a private freight company specialized in expediting the needs of commercial freight companies. Founded in 2019, The Cargo Crew is quickly earning the trust of the freighting and logistics industry as being a reliable asset to their company. The Cargo Crew aspires to being an industry leader and providing exemplary service.

meet the owner

huy

nguyen

Huy Nguyen is no stranger to success as both a business owner and competitive player in Houston market. Though that was not always the case. Like most immigrants family, he grew up struggling socially and and economically. Huy eventually found himself in the wrong crowd making bad decisions. Unable to get a job he knew he had to make some serious life changes. Huy finally saw the light at the end of the tunnel and unknown to him at the time it would change his life around. He started to work as an AC installer at only $8 an hour and learned the trade inside and out. In 2016 he risked his entire savings and pursued his dream of owning a business. He started AMS Coldpro and and solidfied himself as a trusted HVAC company in Houston. Determined and hungry for even more he founded The Cargo Crew. A true embodiment of the entrepreneur's spirit. He aspires to one day tell his story and inspire others to follow their dreams too.
